Node.js & Express.js Development

Job Title: Node.js Developer

In this role, you will develop server-side applications using Node.js and Express.js. You'll focus on building scalable, high-performance web services and integrating them with various data sources.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement server-side applications using Node.js and Express.js.
  • Develop RESTful APIs and manage server-side logic.
  • Integrate with databases and other services.
  • Optimize performance and ensure reliability of applications.
  • Collaborate with frontend developers and other team members.

Scope of Opportunity

This position provides opportunities to work on modern web technologies and large-scale applications. It offers potential growth into senior development roles or specialization in backend technologies.
