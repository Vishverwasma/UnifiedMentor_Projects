C/C++ Development

Job Title: C/C++ Developer

In this role, you will develop and maintain software applications using C and C++. You will work on performance-critical systems and contribute to the design and implementation of various software solutions.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and optimize C/C++ applications and libraries.
  • Analyze and troubleshoot software issues and performance bottlenecks.
  • Collaborate with other developers and teams to deliver high-quality software.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to software architecture discussions.
  • Maintain and update existing codebases as needed.

Scope of Opportunity

This position offers opportunities to work on high-performance and system-level software. It can lead to advanced roles in software engineering, system architecture, or embedded systems development.
